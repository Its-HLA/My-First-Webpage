Defintion of Virtual Reality
 
  A computer-generated environment with scenes and objects that 
  appear to be real, making the user feel they are immersed in 
  their surroundings. 
 
  Essential tools that virtual reality is not complete without :
    
 
  • VR Headset
  • Motion Controllers
  • Tracking SensorsHigh-Performance Computer or Console
  • Haptic Feedback Devices3D Audio Equipment
  • Development Software
  • Content Platforms
  • Safety Features
  • Multiplayer and Social Integration Tools
  • vUser Interface (UI) Tools
  • Analytics and User Feedback Tools
